Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 5, 2026 — LinkToDish

Overview

LinkToDish is designed with your privacy in mind. Your recipes are yours — we don't collect, track, or sell your data.

Data Storage

All recipe data (titles, ingredients, steps, photos, nutrition, categories) is stored locally on your device. If you have iCloud enabled, your data syncs privately across your Apple devices through your personal iCloud account. We have no access to your iCloud data.

Network Access

LinkToDish connects to the internet only when:

  • Importing a recipe from a URL — The app fetches the web page you provide to extract recipe data. Only the URL you enter is accessed.
  • iCloud sync — If enabled on your device, Apple's CloudKit syncs your recipes across your devices through your private iCloud account.

No data is sent to us or any third party.

Camera and Photo Library

LinkToDish requests access to your camera (iOS only) and photo library solely to let you attach photos to your recipes. Photos are stored locally on your device and in your private iCloud account if sync is enabled. We never access, upload, or process your photos on any external server.

Data Collection

LinkToDish does not collect:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or diagnostics
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Health or fitness data

We do not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking frameworks.

Third-Party Services

LinkToDish uses no third-party SDKs or services. The app is built entirely with Apple frameworks.

Data Sharing

We do not share any data with third parties. Period.

Children's Privacy

LinkToDish does not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, we will post the revised version on this page with an updated effective date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, visit our Support Page.
